Practical Mapping with Open Data

Date Saturday 18 July 2026
Time 9:30 — 16:30
Venue The Maltings, Norwich

AboutWhat we'll do

A hands-on day for beginners and improvers. By the end of the workshop you'll have built and published your own interactive map using freely available data — no prior experience required, just a laptop and curiosity.

Lunch, tea and coffee are provided. Bring your own laptop; chargers and Wi-Fi are available on site.
