AboutWhat we'll do

A two-day workshop discussing obstacles and sharing best-practices in producing FAIR data and sharing FAIR workflows at facilities within UK life science institutes.

For esearch technical professionals working at facilities within UK life science institutes.

Whether you are a data producer (lab technician at a sequencing/mass spec/microscopy/other facility), data handler (data steward/data scientist/bioinformatician/other), or facility manager we want your voice and experience heard.

Lunch, tea and coffee are provided.
